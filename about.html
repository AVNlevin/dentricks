Meet Dr. Rikin Shah

Over 17 Years of Dedicated Dental Excellence in Surat

Dr. Rikin Shah (BDS) is the founder and lead dentist of Dentricks Dental Clinic. Known for his precise skills, meticulous attention to detail, and a gentle clinic manner, he has transformed thousands of smiles across Surat since establishing the clinic.

To deliver world-class dental care, Dr. Shah has completed advanced international training in state-of-the-art procedures:

  • Advanced Laser Dentistry (Painless soft & hard tissue treatments)
  • Modern Guided Implantology (Single & full arch reconstructions)
  • Invisalign & Clear Aligner Ortho alignment therapies

Our Philosophy

The Dentricks Promise

Our logo reflects our three core clinical principles that shape every patient treatment.

Precise

Using 3D intraoral imaging, digital planning, and guided laser tools to ensure every restoration, implant, and aligner is placed with absolute micron-level accuracy.

Meticulous

Upholding strict, multi-stage sterilization protocols that exceed global standards, combined with careful cosmetic shading to replicate natural dental aesthetics.

Gentle

Designing anxiety-free experiences. Our advanced dental lasers minimize vibration, sound, and physical contact, allowing for painless procedures and fast healing times.
